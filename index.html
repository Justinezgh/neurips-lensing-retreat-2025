About This Retreat

From Challenge to Paper

We participated in the NeurIPS 2025 Weak Lensing Uncertainty Challenge — a competition at the intersection of cosmology and machine learning, aimed at advancing inference methods for weak gravitational lensing analyses.

Our team developed a strategy that earned us second place. This retreat is our dedicated sprint to transform those results into a full scientific paper — combining focused writing sessions with the rejuvenating backdrop of Orford, Québec.

Community

Code of Conduct

🤝

Be Inclusive

We welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experience levels. Foster an environment where everyone feels valued and heard.

💬

Communicate Constructively

Offer feedback that is specific, actionable, and kind. Critique ideas, not people. Ask questions openly and without judgement.

🔬

Respect the Science

Engage honestly with data and results. Acknowledge uncertainties, give credit generously, and maintain scientific integrity.

🏔️

Rest & Balance

Good science requires a rested mind. Respect everyone's need for breaks, sleep, and time in nature.

🚫

Zero Harassment

Harassment, discrimination, and exclusionary behaviour of any kind are not tolerated. Report concerns to the organisers immediately.

🌿

Leave No Trace

Respect the cottage, the park, and the natural environment. Leave every space cleaner than you found it.
