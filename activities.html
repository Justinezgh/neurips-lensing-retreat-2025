⛷️ Fun Activities

Snowshoeing & Cross-Country Skiing — Parc du Mont-Orford (SEPAQ)

The Parc national du Mont-Orford offers beautifully maintained snowshoe and cross-country ski trails through boreal forest and open meadows. Equipment rental is available directly at the park.

🥾 Snowshoes — $19.99 / day 🎿 XC Skis — $31.50 / day
Park visitor guide (PDF) →
🏔️

Backcountry Ski Touring (Ski de Rando) — Vélo Café Endurance

Vélo Café Endurance rents alpine touring (randonnée) skis — skins included — for climbing the mountain and descending fresh snow. Book ahead online as availability is limited.

Alpine Skiing — Mont Orford (right next to the cottage)

The most convenient option — ski lifts are a short drive from the cottage. Great for a quick afternoon session without losing too much writing time.
