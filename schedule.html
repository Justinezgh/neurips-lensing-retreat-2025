Daily Rhythm

📅 Schedule

Each day follows a structured but flexible rhythm: focused writing in the morning, outdoor activities in the afternoon, a second work block in the evening.

Typical Day

8:00 – 8:30
Breakfast

Breakfast Block

Shared breakfast.

08:30 – 09:00
Check-in

Morning Goals Session

Everyone shares one or two concrete goals for the day.

09:00 – 13:00
Work

Morning Work Block

Focused writing, coding, and analysis. Deep-work mode — minimal interruptions.

13:00 – 17:00
Lunch & Outdoors

Afternoon Activities

Skiing, snowshoeing, ski touring — whatever the group decides. See the activities page for options and rental info.

17:00 – 19:30
Dinner

Dinner Block

Making dinner and eating together.

19:30 - 20:00
Check-in

After-Dinner Progress Share

Brief stand-up after eating. What did you accomplish? What's blocking you?

20:00 – 22:00
Work

Evening Work Block

A second focused session — writing, reviewing, or polishing the morning's output.

Note: Times are approximate and will flex with conditions. The structure is a guide, not a constraint.
